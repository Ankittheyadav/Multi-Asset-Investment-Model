Equity(Stocks)

Equity refers to buying shares of a company, giving you part ownership and the potential for high returns as the company grows.

Mutual Funds

Mutual funds pool money from many investors to invest in a mix of stocks, bonds, or other assets, offering an easy way to diversify and grow your wealth.

Real Estate

Real estate investment involves buying property to earn rental income or sell at a higher value, offering long-term growth and financial security.

Gold

Gold is a safe and trusted investment that protects your wealth during uncertain times and market fluctuations

Fixed Deposits (FDs)

Fixed Deposits offer a safe and guaranteed way to earn interest on your money over a set period, with minimal risk.

Public Provident Fund (PPF)

The Public Provident Fund (PPF) is a safe, government-backed savings scheme that offers attractive interest rates and tax benefits for long-term financial growth.

About Multi-Asset Model

Welcome to our Multi-Asset Investment Model

Our primary goal is to demystify investment options and provide a clear, accessible framework for understanding how different assets can work for you.

What is the Multi-Asset Investment Model?

Investing can often seem overwhelming, especially for beginners. Our model simplifies the process by showcasing various asset classes, including equities, mutual funds, real estate, gold, fixed deposits, and public provident funds (PPF). Each asset class has its unique characteristics, and our model helps you explore these options in a user-friendly manner.

Learn at Your Own Pace

With our interactive calculators, you can input different investment amounts to see how each asset class performs over time. This hands-on approach allows you to understand potential average returns and make informed decisions based on your financial goals.

Why Multi-Asset Investing?

Diversifying your investments across multiple asset classes can help manage risk and improve returns. Our model empowers you to learn the fundamentals of multi-asset investing, providing insights into how different investments can complement each other in your portfolio.
